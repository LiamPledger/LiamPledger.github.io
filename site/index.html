Liam Pledger

Post-doctoral researcher at ETH Zürich

Welcome to my research website. I am a researcher in the chair of Structural Dynamics and Earthquake Engineering at ETH Zürich, with experience in large-scale dynamic and static testing, design and assessment of RC structures, numerical analyses and modelling, field reconnaissance, and structural health monitoring.

This website contains information about my academic background, a list of my publications, and open-source machine learning models developed to estimate drift capacity of RC sections. Supplementary material and resources from my PhD thesis and publications are also included, such as python scripts, unprocessed experimental data, and building models used for conducting incremental dynamic analysis.
